      <h2>Step-by-step playbook</h2>

      <ol class="steps">
        <li>
          <h4>Step 1 — Pick a niche with demand</h4>
          <p>Validate with keyword volume, existing creators, and simple tests (short-form posts, polls). Aim for a niche where you can offer a unique angle.</p>
        </li>

        <li>
          <h4>Step 2 — Create a one-month content plan</h4>
          <p>Plan 3 pillar pieces + 12 micro pieces. Use one pillar to drive subscribers, and micro pieces to grow reach.</p>
        </li>

        <li>
          <h4>Step 3 — Build an owned audience</h4>
          <p>Start collecting emails from day one. A small, engaged email list converts far better than a large, passive follower base.</p>
        </li>

        <li>
          <h4>Step 4 — Monetise early and ethically</h4>
          <p>Test multiple revenue streams: affiliate links, small paid products, consulting calls. Price low and iterate fast.</p>
        </li>

        <li>
          <h4>Step 5 — Systematise and scale</h4>
          <p>Document your production workflow (templates, batch creation), start outsourcing, and reinvest earnings into paid growth when ROI is proven.</p>
        </li>
      </ol>

      <div class="quick-tips">
        <h3>Quick tactics</h3>

          <li>Offer a low-priced product as a “tripwire” to convert cold traffic.</li>
        </ul>
